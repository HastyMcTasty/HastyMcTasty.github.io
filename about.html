About myself

I'm a freelance photographer and web designer in Kassel, Germany.

When I'm not out taking pictures or designing websites, I'm in university, studying to become a teacher here in Germany.

I'm not yet a full stack designer but I am constantly in the progress of improving my skills and mastering my crafts.
